The Slingshot

The slingshot can is as a device used to optimize the displacement of objects, so it's basically a device used to throw projectiles further than some humans are a capable of.

The slingshot may be seen as an outdated piece of technology but the main concept behind can be expanded in so many ways and can also contribute to future technologies. Like, we went from the idea of launching small stones at ourselves to launching huge rockets into space, though both seem very different, their both still just object propulsion. Though there are many ways to propel objects, the slingshot makes use of elastic materials.

Hacking The Sling Shot

In a slingshot momentum is generated and transfered from the system to the projectile. The system creates momentum by utilizing
the force generated from the expansion and contraction of an elastic band. When the elastic band is extended it gains a potential energy, and when it is released the potential energy gets converted to kinetic energy
which means it now has a velocity which also means that momentum is being generated.
Elastic potential energy = 1/2 x k x extension(x)2
kinetic energy = 1/2 x mass x velocity2
PE = KE => KX2/2 = mv2
Therefore velocity(v)= (kx2/m)1/2
Using the formula above we could find ways to maximize velocity, but its a bit to complicated.

So an alternative formula is;
Momentum = mass x velocity = Force x velocity / Acceleration = Force / Change in Time.
From this equation we now understand that the relationship between force and momentum is direct, so an increase in force causes
and increase in momentum. And as I've mentioned before the force generated is due to elasticity. Using this formula;
Force = k x extension
We can say that,
Momentum = k x extension / change in time.
Using this new formula we can increase momentum directly by increasing extension.
extension = final length - original lenght
A reduction in the original length not only causes an increase in extension but it also makes the system more portable. The final
lenght is most important, as it is the lenght the elastic band can stretch without breaking and it depends mainly on the material used.
An increase in final lenght would also cause an increase in extension.

An Electromagnetic Slingshot

This design incoporates three magnets in total, one permanent magnet and two electromangnets. The permatent magnet is made moveable in an air sealed chamber, while The two solenoids placed at the ends, alternate their magnetic fields causing the permanent magnet to oscillate back and forth. As one solenoid attracts the magnet the other repels it, which in my head i believe might increase its velocity, and also in my head, I believe that as the permanent magnet moves towards one side it forces the air in that section to move through the tight air passage and out into the other section, thus pushing the magnet even more.
I don't a have to materials to recreate this, so theory that the pressurized air would cause any increase in velocity is very sceptical.

Though my second design utilisies the pressurized air generated to move the projectile. A vent is included in the system to prevent a vacuum from being created and opposing the movement of the magnet.
