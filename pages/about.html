About

The main purpose of this website is to educate and share my ideas on various phenomena and technological advancements in our society. I also intend for this website to be more of a blog where I explore ideas on the optimization of humanities current technological advancements. I have no current outstanding degrees yet but I am a high school student who has studied deeply into most of these subjects.

This website was created so that I could share not only my ideas on various concepts but also educate people in way that i believe is more practical than some text books. I do this by studying various activities that go on in the world and concepts and trying my best to break them down into various physics topics. I do this because I think it helps us understand not only the topics more, but the world around us and also helps us find ways to improve it.
